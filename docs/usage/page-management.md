# Page & Section Management

## Pages

### Creating Pages
Click **+ Add page** at the bottom of the pages panel. A new "Untitled" page is created and the rename dialog opens automatically.

### Renaming Pages
Right-click a page → **Rename**, or double-click the page name. Press **Enter** to confirm or **Escape** to cancel.

### Deleting Pages
Right-click a page → **Delete**. The page is removed and the next page in the section loads.

### Moving Pages
Drag a page onto a section name to move it to that section.

### Sorting Pages
Click the sort icon (⇅) in the pages header to choose:

- **Alphabetical** (A-Z or Z-A)
- **Date Created** (newest or oldest first)
- **Date Modified** (newest or oldest first)

Sort preference is saved per section.

---

## Sections

### Creating Sections
Click **+ Add section** at the bottom of the sections panel.

### Renaming Sections
Right-click a section → **Rename**. The display name is stored in `.section.md` frontmatter.

### Setting Section Color
Right-click a section → **Set Color**. Choose from 8 preset colors or clear the color.

### Deleting Sections
Right-click a section → **Delete**.

---

## Protected Sections

Sections named `1-todo` and `1-weeks` are protected:
- Cannot be renamed or deleted
- Pages can still be moved to/from them
